# TrackVolt — Competitor Comparison
**Date:** March 1, 2026

## Overview
TrackVolt is a hybrid athlete PWA combining CrossFit tracking, HYROX logging, nutrition, cycle tracking, and recovery in one app. We compare against 6 specialized competitors.

## 1. BTWB (Beyond the Whiteboard) — CrossFit Tracking

### What BTWB Does BETTER:
- **Gym affiliation** — connects to your box, sees programmed WODs automatically
- **Percentile rankings** — shows how you compare to other athletes (e.g., "Fran: top 15%")
- **Movement library** — comprehensive video demos with scaling options
- **Workout sharing** — social feed, comment on gym mates' scores
- **Historical benchmark tracking** — graphs your Fran time over 5 years
- **Competition mode** — leaderboards for in-gym competitions
- **Coach tools** — coaches can program and see athlete compliance

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **Integrated nutrition** — BTWB has zero nutrition tracking
- **Cycle tracking** — BTWB has no women's health features
- **HYROX support** — BTWB is CrossFit-only
- **Offline-first** — BTWB requires constant internet
- **Free** — BTWB costs $8/month
- **Privacy** — all data local, BTWB stores everything in cloud
- **Recovery score** — BTWB has no recovery tracking

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- Social/community features
- Gym WOD auto-population
- Percentile rankings for benchmarks
- Video movement library

## 2. SugarWOD — CrossFit Social

### What SugarWOD Does BETTER:
- **Social feed** — see gym mates' scores, fist-bump reactions
- **Coach programming** — coaches post daily WODs, athletes log results
- **Leaderboard** — daily/weekly/monthly leaderboards per gym
- **Movement standards** — video of each movement
- **Comment threads** — discuss WODs with community
- **Photo sharing** — post workout selfies

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **Nutrition tracking** — SugarWOD has none
- **HYROX + Running** — SugarWOD is CrossFit-only
- **Body metrics** — weight, sleep, energy, body measurements
- **PR Wall** — more sophisticated PR tracking
- **Cycle tracking** — not available in SugarWOD
- **1RM calculator** — built-in
- **Barcode food scanner** — built-in

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- Social features (the #1 reason people use SugarWOD)
- Gym connection

## 3. MyFitnessPal — Nutrition

### What MFP Does BETTER:
- **Food database** — 14M+ verified foods vs our limited set
- **Barcode reliability** — MFP's scanner works on 95%+ of products
- **Meal planning** — full meal plans, recipes, macro goals per meal
- **Restaurant menus** — chain restaurant items pre-loaded
- **Community recipes** — user-submitted meals with macros
- **Diary history** — years of nutrition data with trends
- **Premium insights** — weekly nutrition reports, food patterns

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **Workout tracking** — MFP's exercise logging is basic
- **Athlete-focused macros** — our formula uses CrossFit-specific multipliers
- **All-in-one** — MFP requires separate apps for workouts, sleep, etc.
- **No ads** — MFP free tier is ad-heavy
- **Privacy** — MFP shares data with third parties (Under Armour ecosystem)
- **Offline** — MFP requires internet for food search

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- Massive food database (critical gap)
- Restaurant menu items
- Recipe builder with macro breakdown
- Meal planning features

## 4. Flo — Cycle Tracking

### What Flo Does BETTER:
- **Prediction accuracy** — ML-based period prediction, improves over time with logged data
- **Symptom patterns** — detects your personal patterns over months
- **Educational content** — articles, videos about each phase
- **Pregnancy mode** — full pregnancy tracking with weekly updates
- **TTC mode** — trying to conceive with ovulation prediction
- **Partner sharing** — share cycle info with partner
- **Community** — anonymous discussion forums
- **Health reports** — shareable PDFs for doctor visits
- **14+ years of data** — trust and track record

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **Training sync** — Flo has no workout integration (this is our unique selling point)
- **Phase-based training recommendations** — Dr. Stacy Sims research-backed
- **Recovery score with cycle factor** — no competitor does this
- **Athlete focus** — Flo is general women's health, we're athlete-first
- **Privacy** — all local vs Flo's $56M privacy fine history
- **Integrated** — one app instead of Flo + separate training app
- **RED-S screening** — no period tracker checks for athletic energy deficiency

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- ML-based prediction (not just fixed math)
- Educational content for each phase
- Pregnancy tracking (we have the type but no UI)
- Doctor-shareable health reports

## 5. Strava — Running/Cardio

### What Strava Does BETTER:
- **GPS tracking** — live route mapping with elevation
- **Segments** — compare your route times vs all athletes
- **Social** — kudos, comments, clubs, challenges
- **Training load** — fitness/freshness graphs
- **Device integration** — Garmin, Wahoo, Apple Watch sync
- **Route planning** — discover new routes
- **Live tracking** — share location with safety contacts

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **CrossFit/HYROX** — Strava has no WOD logging
- **Nutrition** — Strava has none
- **Strength tracking** — Strava is cardio-only
- **Cycle integration** — Strava has none
- **Offline** — Strava requires GPS/internet

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- GPS route tracking (major gap for runners)
- Device sync (Garmin, Apple Watch)
- Training load analysis
- Social features

## 6. WHOOP — Recovery

### What WHOOP Does BETTER:
- **24/7 biometric monitoring** — HRV, resting HR, sleep stages automatically
- **Strain score** — precise daily exertion measurement
- **Recovery score** — data-driven, not self-reported
- **Sleep coach** — tells you optimal bedtime
- **Journal** — correlate behaviors with recovery
- **Trend analysis** — months of objective data

### What TrackVolt Does BETTER:
- **Workout logging** — WHOOP tracks strain but not specific exercises
- **Nutrition** — WHOOP has none
- **Free** — WHOOP costs $30/month + hardware
- **No hardware required** — TrackVolt is software-only
- **More accessible** — WHOOP requires wearing a strap 24/7
- **Cycle tracking** — WHOOP's women's health is basic

### Missing Features Users Expect:
- Wearable device integration
- Objective recovery data (HRV, auto sleep tracking)
- Strain scoring
